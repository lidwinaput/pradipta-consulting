I'm Lidwina Putri, an Indonesian sustainability and human rights professional based in Paris. My work starts from a simple conviction: the data only means something if you understand what it represents in human terms.

I've done fieldwork with coastal communities displaced by climate change in Indonesia. I've also consolidated Scope 1–3 emissions across 43 countries and coordinated CDP reporting for a global company. Most people are one or the other. I've been both.

That combination — field-grounded, institutionally fluent — is what Pradipta Consulting brings to every engagement.
